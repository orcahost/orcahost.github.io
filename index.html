What is Orca?

Orca is a simple and easy interface to link your social accounts and online platforms in one location. One link.

It is designed to be an all in one solution for all your networking needs for your social media accounts and online portfolios to enable users to access of their friends Orca accounts to connect easily.

How do I use it?

Orca is extremely easy to get started with! For social medias, just enter your handles.
If you have custom links, just choose one of the following that’s not a specific platform and copy & paste your URL.

Who’s it for?

Orca is for everyone! No matter who you are, you can find a use with Orca.
It’s not just for social media websites. You can link articles, you own spotify playlist, your github portfolio, web design link, Uber profile, Yelp review - The possibilities are endless!
